## Strength progression

- +2.5 kg/session until failure in 5x5 (DL +5 kg on top set)
- Two consecutive failed sessions on same lift → -10% and restart
- Expected linear progression: 4-6 weeks upper, 6-8 weeks lower, before needing periodization
